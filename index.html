My June 2016 Puerto Rico Trip

My high school Spanish teacher invited kids from her classes to go on a trip to Puerto Rico this summer. She wanted us to have a chance to travel and experience new things. I gladly accepted the invitation. Several months have passed since we first signed up for the trip, and now the trip is almost upon us and I am SO EXCITED! We leave on June 27th and return on July 2nd.


DAY 1 - JUNE 27

We have to be at the Nashville Airport at 4am on Monday morning. Our first flight departs at 6:45am to Atlanta. We should arrive at Atlanta at 8:54am. Our second flight departs at 9:55am to San Juan, Puerto Rico. We should arrive at San Juan at 1:30pm. After we land, we will meet our tour director, Astacio Santiago, and check into our hotel, Hyatt Place. After we get settles in, we are going to walk the city and see: Museo de Casa Blanca, La Muralla, and La Fortaleza. When we are finished walking, we are going to eat dinner.

DAY 2 - JUNE 28

On Tuesday, we are gonna start the day with breakfast then we are going on a Zipline Canopy Tour. After we finish our zipling, we are going on a guided sightseeing tour to visit: Antiguo Casino, El Castillo San Cristobal, El Castillo del Morro, San Juan Museum, Convento de Santo Tomás, Iglésia San Jose, Catedral de San Juan, and Pablo Casals Museum. After all our sightseeing is over, it will be time for dinner.

DAY 3 - JUNE 29

Wednesday starts off with breakfast and then a visit to the El Yunque Caribbean National Forest. Then we will travel to Luquillo beach, via the coastal route, for some free time. When we are done at the beach or maybe while we are at the beach, we will eat dinner. After dark, we are going to do a Bioluminescent Bay excursion.

DAY 4 - JUNE 30

Thursday starts off with breakfast and then the rest of the day will be spent doing island excursion and snorkeling. When we are done, it will be time for dinner.

DAY 5 - JULY 1

Friday starts off with breakfast and then Camuy Cave guided excursion. After we are done there, we are packing our bags traveling to Ponce. We will then check into the Ponce Plaza Hotel and Casino. After we check in, we will visit the Tibes Ceremonial Park. Then we will have a guided Ponce sightseeing tour of: Parque de Bombas, Lions Fountain, Ponce Cathedral, Casa Alcaldia. After we finish sightseeing, we will eat dinner.

DAY 6 - JULY 2

Saturday starts with breakfast and then we have to pack up and travel back to San Juan. Today we are going home so we have to be at the San Juan Airport around 3pm. Our first flight departs at 4:50pm to Atlanta. We should arrive at Atlanta at 8:54pm. Our second flight should depart at 10:10pm from Atlanta to Nashville. We should arrive at the Nashville Airport at 10:21pm. Then I have a 2 hour drive home. So I should get home around Midnight.
